Formerly known as <Affinity>, our community has been raiding on Wyrmrest since 2013.

Our only focus when making our guild originally was having a relaxed atmosphere to clear Normal raids with, and have fun while doing it.
Over the years, we improved into a unique guild that cleared Mythic while retaining a pristine environment. We earned Cutting Edge 14 times.

Now, we're taking a step back and focusing on Heroic raiding with a casual mindset.

Details

  • Only 2 Hours a week! Tuesdays from 9:00pm to 11:00pm Pacific Time

  • Our primary focus is on our anxiety and stress free environment.

  • Raiders will never be forced to sit. No mandatory benching.

  • You will never be forced to reroll off of the class you apply on.

  • No Research requirements, but learning is expected.

You should apply if:

  • You're looking for a chill community to raid in for fun, not just to win.

  • You're looking for a guild that treats members fairly, regardless of race, gender, or sexual orientation.

  • You're mainly interested in raiding.

  • You treat everyone, in-game and out, with respect.

You should not apply if:

  • Terms like 'LGBTQ+' and 'Pronouns' scare you. <Life> does not tolerate hateful ideology.

  • You're mainly looking for Keystones or other aspects of the game. <Life> is primarily a raiding guild with no Keystone expectations.

  • You're looking for a large or always active guild. Expect a majority of activity during our raid day and in Discord.

  • You're looking for something more serious or competitive. Even when <Life> was raiding Mythic, we aimed to be average.

  • Apply!
