Formerly known as <Affinity>, our community has been raiding on Wyrmrest since September 2013.
Our only focus when making our guild was having a relaxed atmosphere to clear Normal raids with, and have fun while doing it.
Over the years, we have improved into a unique guild that clears Mythic while retaining a pristine environment. We've earned Cutting Edge 14 times.

Details

  • 9 Hours a week - Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday from 7:00pm to 10:00pm Pacific

  • Our primary focus is on our anxiety and stress-free environment.

  • Core Raiders will never be forced to sit. No mandatory benching.

  • You will never be forced to reroll off of the class you apply on.

  • No Research requirements, but quick learning is expected.

You should apply if:

  • You're looking for a chill community to raid in for fun, not just to win.

  • You're looking for a guild that treats members fairly, regardless of race, gender, or sexual orientation.

  • You like the idea of raid logging & not being forced to do much content outside of the raid.

  • You treat everyone, in-game and out, with respect.

You should not apply if:

  • Terms like 'LGBTQ+' and 'Pronouns' scare you.

  • You're mainly looking for Keystones or other aspects of the game. <Life> is primarily a Raiding Guild with little Keystone expectations.

  • You're looking for a guild that is active outside of raids through the entire season. <Life> encourages Raid Logging to avoid burnout.

  • You're looking for something more serious or competitive. <Life> aims to be an average Mythic Raiding guild.
